I love you

Tu chandni ki aakhri kiran hai, Main raat ke geet ka behta svar hoon. Teri baat mein ek purn viram hai, Meri soch abhi bhi adhura prashn hai.

❦

Tu bikharta hai jaise bhor ka sapna, Main thahar jaoon to rang pheeka lage. Teri raftaar mein ek safar basa hai, Meri manzil pe rukna bhi ajeeb lage.

❧

Tu bhanwar mein thami ek kashti hai, Main kinara jo haathon se fisal gaya. Teri aankhon mein ujale ki halchal hai, Meri palkon pe ek khwab pal gaya.

❧

Tu mehka hua ek basanti jhonka, Main patjhad ki baasi hawaa hoon. Teri hasi mein suraj ki taqat hai, Main shaam ka pighalta dhuan hoon.

❧

Tu shabd hai jo geet mein dhal chuka, Main soch hoon jo ab tak beh rahi. Tu aakhri pankti ka thehra sa sukoon, Main kavita jo ab tak keh rahi.

❧

Tere hone se har lafz ek mehka geet hai, Meri zubaan pe bas tera hi preet hai. Tere saath zindagi ek ghazal ki chaap hai, Tere bina har pal bas ek suna sa aabshaar hai.

❧

Tu kisi baag ka ek khilta gulshan, Main woh khushbu jo hawaaon mein likhi dastan. Teri yaadon ki barish mein bheegta hoon, Meri har boond sirf tere naam likhti hai.

❧

Teri yaadon ki chaandni mein bheegta hoon, Meri raaton ka har andhera dhalta hai. Tere saath har pal ek kahani likhta hai, Tere bina har alfaaz adhura lagta hai.
